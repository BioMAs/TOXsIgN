Welcome to TOXsIgN

The TOXicogenomic sIgNature (TOXsIgN) repository provides a flexible and open design that facilitates online submission by the toxicology community, storage and retrieval of toxicogenomic signatures, defined as the description of (transcript-/epigen-)omics effects on individuals or their descendants, after exposure to single or combined environmental factors, including chemical (e.g. pesticides, plasticizers, drugs, endocrine disruptors), physical (e.g. radiations, temperature) or biological (e.g. pathogens, parasites) factors. TOXsIgN also intends to serve as a warehouse for toxicogenomics and predictive toxicology tools which could lean on the deposited signatures deposited by the community.
