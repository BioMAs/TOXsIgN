Register

In order to register yourself in TOXsIgN, you need to click Register button in the top right hand corner of TOXsIgN.

It will take you to a screen where you need provide :

  • A first name
  • A last name
  • An institute name
  • An email address (which will be your account login)
  • An institute address
  • A country
  • A password

When complete click the Register button in the bottom. After you have registered your information you will need to activate your account. You will receive an email in the email account you have provided.
